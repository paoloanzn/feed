### Main Topic: Nationalism and Economics

**Main Thesis:** A nation can support economic freedom and political liberty if it is peaceful and respects the right of people to choose their own destiny.

**Simple Summary:** Ludwig von Mises argued that a country can be both nationalistic and economically free. This is possible only if the nation is peaceful and allows individuals to decide their own future.

**The Bottom Line:** > For a country to have a healthy economy and free people, it must prioritize peace and respect the freedom of its people.
