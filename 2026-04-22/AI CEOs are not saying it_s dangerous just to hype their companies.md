### Main Topic: AI Safety Claims

**Main Thesis:** The idea that AI danger warnings are just marketing is unlikely because major companies and researchers have taken costly, public actions to sound the alarm.

**Simple Summary:** Many people argue that big tech companies talk about AI danger only to make money. This article argues against that idea. It points out that the movement for AI safety started long before these companies existed, and many companies actually spent money and made structural changes (like being non-profits) to try and reduce AI risk. Furthermore, key researchers and employees have made personal sacrifices, like quitting or giving up money, to publicly warn about the dangers of advanced AI.

**The Bottom Line:** > The fact that people are making costly moves to warn about AI danger suggests that the risks are real, not just a sales tactic.
