### Main Topic: Taxation and Liberty

**Main Thesis:** Income taxes and mandatory government service violate natural rights and are incompatible with a free society.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that the system of income taxes and mandatory government service (like the military draft) is fundamentally unfair because it implies that individuals' rights are not inherent but are granted by the government and can be taken away. The author connects this idea to the concept of "withholding" earnings, asserting that these systems are incompatible with true personal freedom and liberty.

**The Bottom Line:** > To value freedom and peace, people must reject income taxation and mandatory government service because these systems violate fundamental individual rights.
