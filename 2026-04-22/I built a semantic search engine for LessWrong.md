### Main Topic: Information Linking

**Main Thesis:** The author built a tool to connect similar documents from online discussions to help people navigate and understand complex ideas.

**Simple Summary:** The author created a system that searches a massive database of online posts (like those on LessWrong or Substack) and finds the 100 most similar documents to any given link. This tool uses advanced AI to understand the meaning of the text and connect related ideas, aiming to help newcomers quickly catch up on complex discussions in fields like AI safety.

**The Bottom Line:** > This tool is valuable because it helps newcomers quickly find context and understand how new ideas fit into existing knowledge, speeding up learning in rapidly changing fields.
