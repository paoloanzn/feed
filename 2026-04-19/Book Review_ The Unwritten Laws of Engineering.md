### Main Topic: Systematic Thinking

**Main Thesis:** To gain control over your life, you must learn to apply the logical, systematic thinking used in your work to your personal life.

**Simple Summary:** Many people struggle to use logical, organized thinking in their personal lives, even though they are experts in their careers. The author argues that books like *The Unwritten Laws of Engineering* teach a method for shifting from messy, emotional thinking to clear, systematic thinking. This involves treating yourself like a system, just like an engineer treats a project, to make better decisions and manage your life effectively.

**The Bottom Line:** > Applying a systematic approach to how you think about yourself is the key to taking control of your life and achieving personal success.
