### Main Topic: Information and AI

**Main Thesis:** Using AI to find truth might increase the correlation of information, which could make the world less accurate and robust.

**Simple Summary:** The article discusses how finding useful information often requires looking at sources that disagree with each other (low correlation). The author suggests using AI to help people figure out what is true. However, the author worries that AI systems might unintentionally increase the correlation between information, meaning everyone will agree more, which could hide important, differing viewpoints and make the overall understanding of the world less reliable.

**The Bottom Line:** > If AI increases how much information agrees with itself, we risk losing the ability to find necessary, diverse, and corrective viewpoints.
