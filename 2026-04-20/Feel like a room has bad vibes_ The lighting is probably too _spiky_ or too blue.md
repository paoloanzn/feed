### Main Topic: Lighting Quality

**Main Thesis:** The quality of indoor lighting, specifically the color and spectrum of the light, is a major factor in how a space feels, and upgrading to high-quality light bulbs can restore a natural feeling to a home.

**Simple Summary:** How a room feels is heavily influenced by the light it receives. The article explains that poor lighting, especially from modern LED bulbs, makes spaces feel artificial. To fix this, you need to focus on two things: Color Rendering Index (CRI), which measures how accurately a light shows colors, and Color Temperature (Kelvin), which determines if the light is warm (red/yellow) or cold (blue). Using light bulbs with a high CRI (95 or higher) and choosing warm color temperatures (2000K-3000K) will make your home feel more natural and comfortable.

**The Bottom Line:** > To make your home feel more natural and comfortable, stop ignoring lighting quality and invest in high-CRI, warm-toned light bulbs.
