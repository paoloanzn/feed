### Main Topic: Sleep Habits

**Main Thesis:** Changing your physical environment and increasing activity can significantly improve sleep quality, even when old habits remain.

**Simple Summary:** The author experienced poor sleep before moving to a new location, but sleep improved dramatically after moving to a new environment and making specific lifestyle changes. The main factors that helped were exercising more, separating work and leisure time, and creating physical boundaries between the bedroom and other activities. While habits like screen use and drinking alcohol didn't change, these adjustments made a big difference in overall well-being.

**The Bottom Line:** > To improve sleep, focus on increasing physical activity and creating clear physical and mental boundaries between your sleeping space and your daily life.
