### Main Topic: Political Systems

**Main Thesis:** Representative democracy is flawed because it delegates individual freedom and responsibility, making it an obsolete and imperfect system.

**Simple Summary:** The article argues that representative democracy is not truly democratic because it forces people to delegate their freedom to representatives, which dilutes individual responsibility. This system is outdated because it relies on geography, which no longer reflects how people connect today. The author proposes that the free market is a better form of democracy because it allows every individual's choice (through buying and selling) to directly influence outcomes, linking freedom and accountability.

**The Bottom Line:** > True freedom and responsibility are found not in mass government, but in individual choices within the free market, which is the only system that respects individual liberty.
