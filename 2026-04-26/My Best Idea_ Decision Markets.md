### Main Topic: Decision Markets

**Main Thesis:** Speculative markets can help guide specific decisions by predicting the outcomes of those choices, which could lead to better governance.

**Simple Summary:** The author proposes using speculative markets—markets that predict future outcomes—to advise people on what decisions to make. This idea combines the ability of markets to gather information with the fact that information is most valuable when it helps make specific choices. The ultimate goal is to use these markets to create a system where decisions are more effective, leading to better governance.

**The Bottom Line:** > If markets can help guide decisions, they have the potential to create a world with much more effective and competent governance.
