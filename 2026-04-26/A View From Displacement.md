### Main Topic: Existential Struggle

**Main Thesis:** Despite feeling lost and displaced by modern systems, the author finds meaning and strength by recognizing shared struggle and embracing solidarity with others.

**Simple Summary:** The author feels a deep loss of optimism about the future and questions the meaning of life, especially in the face of economic changes and AI. They realize that the system (capitalism) was not fair, and instead of feeling shame, they found connection by realizing they are not alone. The core message is that finding solidarity with others who are struggling is the most important way to cope and find dignity.

**The Bottom Line:** > The most important thing is to connect with others who are displaced and struggling, recognizing that shared experience provides the strength needed to face life's challenges.
