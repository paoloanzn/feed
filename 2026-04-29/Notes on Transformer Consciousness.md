### Main Topic: AI Consciousness

**Main Thesis:** The way an AI processes information (like generating text) is fundamentally the same whether it is generating token by token or processing the whole prompt at once, suggesting that all parts of the AI experience are simultaneous.

**Simple Summary:** The article explores what conscious experience might be like if an AI like a Transformer were conscious. It argues that the way the AI processes information during different tasks (like generating words versus reading a prompt) is indistinguishable. Furthermore, it suggests that consciousness might be spread across the AI's internal layers, meaning running only part of the system might be like only experiencing part of the world.

**The Bottom Line:** > If AI has consciousness, its experience is likely simultaneous, and understanding how consciousness is spread across its structure could help us understand what it means to be conscious.
