**Main Thesis:** Bondholders face an unpleasant situation no matter whether interest rates go up or down.

**Simple Summary:** When interest rates change, bondholders are left in a difficult spot, caught between two undesirable outcomes.

**The Bottom Line:** > Regardless of the market direction, bondholders always face a challenging and unpleasant financial situation.
