### Main Topic: AI Safety Strategy

**Main Thesis:** To effectively manage the risks of AI, the field needs more people who can combine strategic thinking with the ability to implement real-world solutions.

**Simple Summary:** The article proposes a program called Astra, run by Constellation, to train people in the skills needed for AI safety. It focuses on developing "strong strategic thinking" and "high agency"—the ability to identify problems and then actually implement solutions. The program connects participants with experienced mentors and real-world challenges, helping them move from just thinking about AI risks to actually owning and solving them.

**The Bottom Line:** > To make meaningful progress in AI safety, we need more experts who can not only identify risks but also lead the practical work of fixing them.
