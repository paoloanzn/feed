### Main Topic: AI Welfare Timing

**Main Thesis:** We should not wait until after a major AI breakthrough to focus on AI welfare, as delaying risks locking in bad outcomes.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that while complex AI welfare work seems hard, delaying it until after a massive AI intelligence explosion is risky because we might lock in poor values early on. However, they suggest that instead of focusing on hard technical details now, we should prioritize setting up strategies, policies, and building public understanding to guide the future development of AI.

**The Bottom Line:** > We must start working on AI welfare now to shape the future, even if we cannot solve everything immediately, by focusing on strategy and setting good incentives.
