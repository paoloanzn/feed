### Main Topic: Science Ownership

**Main Thesis:** If scientific knowledge is a public resource, other nations should pay for access to it rather than causing international conflict.

**Simple Summary:** The article argues that science belongs to everyone, not just one country. Instead of arguing about who has access to scientific work, nations like China should pay for the use of that knowledge. This approach avoids conflict and treats science as a shared benefit for the world.

**The Bottom Line:** > Treating science as a shared public good means countries should focus on paying for access rather than fighting over ownership, which promotes global cooperation.
