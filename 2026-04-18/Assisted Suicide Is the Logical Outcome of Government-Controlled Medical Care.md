### Main Topic: Assisted Suicide Policy

**Main Thesis:** The author argues that allowing physician-assisted suicide is a logical way to manage and save a government-controlled medical system while ensuring people can die with dignity.

**Simple Summary:** The article suggests that in a system where the government controls healthcare, allowing assisted suicide is a practical way to manage end-of-life care. This approach is presented as a way to maintain the system and allow patients to choose a dignified end to their lives.

**The Bottom Line:** > This perspective focuses on balancing the needs of the healthcare system with the personal choice of patients regarding their end-of-life care.
