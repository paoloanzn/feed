### Main Topic: AI Model Naming

**Main Thesis:** OpenAI's current system for naming and describing its different AI models is confusing and unhelpful, making it impossible for users to understand the unique value of each model.

**Simple Summary:** Users are frustrated because OpenAI hides important information about its different AI models and provides vague descriptions. The names and descriptions do not clearly explain what makes one model better than another, making it difficult for people to choose the right tool for a specific job. The author argues that the company needs to provide clear, simple explanations detailing the specific strengths of each model.

**The Bottom Line:** > To make AI models useful, companies must stop hiding information and clearly explain exactly what each model is best at so users can make smart choices.
