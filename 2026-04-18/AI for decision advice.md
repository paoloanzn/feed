### Main Topic: AI Advice

**Main Thesis:** When AI gives advice for important decisions, it should focus on guiding the user through the decision process rather than just providing an answer.

**Simple Summary:** The author reviewed how different AI models (like ChatGPT and Gemini) give advice in serious situations. They argue that AI should not just give a detailed analysis, but should instead ask the user questions about how they plan to make the decision (like seeking more information or involving others). Furthermore, AI should proactively point out important ethical considerations that the user might be missing.

**The Bottom Line:** > For AI to be truly helpful in important decisions, it must prioritize transparency and guiding the user through the process, not just offering a final solution.
