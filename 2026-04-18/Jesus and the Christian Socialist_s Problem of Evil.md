### Main Topic: Jesus and Suffering

**Main Thesis:** Using Jesus to justify socialism creates a conflict because it ignores the reality of suffering and the limits of divine power.

**Simple Summary:** The article looks at the conflict between using Jesus as a basis for socialist ideas and the problem of evil. It argues that if ending suffering requires a moral duty, then the Jesus who healed some people but not all seems either unwilling or unable to fix all the pain.

**The Bottom Line:** > You cannot use religious figures to demand social change if they don't fully explain why suffering exists and why it hasn't been eliminated.
