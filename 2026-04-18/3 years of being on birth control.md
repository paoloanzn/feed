### Main Topic: Birth Control Effects

**Main Thesis:** Different birth control options affect mood and energy levels, and the changes are largely explained by how they change a hormone called SHBG.

**Simple Summary:** The author experimented with several types of birth control pills (fourth-generation progestins) to find what worked best for managing periods and symptoms. They observed that the changes in their mood and energy were strongly linked to changes in Sex Hormone Binding Globulin (SHBG), which controls how much testosterone is available in the body. They tried various options, including those using drospirenone and etonogestrel, and found that while some options provided relief, others caused mood issues or physical discomfort.

**The Bottom Line:** > The way birth control affects your mood and energy is primarily driven by changes in SHBG, which is a more important factor than the hormones themselves when predicting side effects.
