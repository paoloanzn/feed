### Main Topic: Money Supply Growth

**Main Thesis:** Despite claims that monetary policy is restrictive, the actual money supply has been growing rapidly, which is surprising given the weak economic conditions.

**Simple Summary:** The article argues that the Federal Reserve (the Fed) insists its policies are restrictive, but data on the actual money supply shows it has been growing quickly. The money supply has increased every month recently, and even though the economy is weak and job growth is slow, the money supply is still growing. This growth is driven by private banks responding to loose Fed policies.

**The Bottom Line:** > The Fed's actions have successfully increased the money supply to growth levels, proving that the money supply is not slowing down as much as official statements suggest.
