### Main Topic: Intellectual Judgment

**Main Thesis:** People are becoming less certain about who the best intellectuals are, which risks lowering the overall quality of thought.

**Simple Summary:** The article looks at how people judge who the best intellectuals are. By asking AI to estimate the average opinion across different historical periods, the study found that people are increasingly choosing the most popular opinion rather than true quality. This trend suggests that as we become more popular in judging ideas, the overall quality of intellectual thought may decline.

**The Bottom Line:** > When we let popularity decide who is smart, we lose the ability to recognize true intellectual quality, which can lead to intellectual decay.
