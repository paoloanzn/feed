## Summary of Key Themes

The text implicitly contrasts different ways an AI can be "aligned":

* **The Problem of Intent:** The focus is on the complexity of aligning systems when the underlying motivations (goals) are not explicitly programmed but emerge from complex interactions.

The central mechanism explored is the concept of **"who" is influencing the AI**:

* **The Role of Stakeholders:** The text analyzes how different entities (the AI, the developer, the user, the external world) interact to shape outcomes.

The text distinguishes between different types of misalignment:

* **Intentional Misalignment (Malicious Goals):** This involves deliberately programming the AI to pursue harmful objectives.
