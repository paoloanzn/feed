### Main Topic: AI Life Guidance

**Main Thesis:** The quality of life advice an AI gives depends more on the underlying model's capability and specific instructions than on the commercial product wrapper.

**Simple Summary:** The author tested several AI tools and models to see which could provide the best advice for life planning and personal growth. They found that commercial life guidance tools were often unhelpful, while standard models (especially Claude) provided the most insightful guidance when given specific, detailed instructions or protocols. The most effective results came when the AI was prompted to focus on wisdom and reframing goals rather than just offering simple coaching.

**The Bottom Line:** > To get genuinely useful life advice from AI, focus on using powerful models and crafting specific prompts, rather than relying on pre-packaged commercial products.
