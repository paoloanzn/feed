### Main Topic: Drug Development Costs

**Main Thesis:** Making new drugs is extremely expensive and inefficient, and we need to fix the way clinical trials and ethics are handled.

**Simple Summary:** Drug development costs are rising despite technological advances. The article discusses why this is happening and asks how we can make clinical trials more efficient, why some companies move trials to other countries, and how to improve ethics boards without sacrificing safety.

**The Bottom Line:** > To develop necessary medicines faster and cheaper, we must reform the rules for clinical trials and ethical oversight.
