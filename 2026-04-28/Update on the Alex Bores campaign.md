### Main Topic: Political Strategy

**Main Thesis:** Donating to Alex Bores's campaign is a highly cost-effective way to influence AI safety policy, and attacks against him have actually increased his influence.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that attacking Alex Bores, a candidate who supports AI safety, has made him a more prominent figure. The goal of the attackers (Leading the Future) is to scare other politicians into avoiding pro-AI safety positions. This strategy has worked because Bores has gained positive media attention, making him seem like an expert on the issue. The author concludes that donations and volunteering are effective ways to support this cause, and that the political attacks have created a beneficial environment for AI safety advocates.

**The Bottom Line:** > Supporting candidates like Bores is a smart, low-cost way to push for AI safety, and political attacks can be used strategically to make important issues more visible.
