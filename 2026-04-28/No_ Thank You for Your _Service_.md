### Main Topic: Military Hypocrisy

**Main Thesis:** The author rejects thanking military personnel because their actions often contradict noble ideals, and the military system itself is fundamentally corrupt and serves evil interests.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that the idea of "thank you for your service" is hypocritical because the military often engages in violent actions that contradict its stated ideals. The author claims the military is not fighting for the country's safety but is instead a tool for global control, covering up crimes, running wars for profit, and using taxpayer money for surveillance and violence. Ultimately, the sacrifices made by soldiers are overshadowed by the structural evil of the government that controls them.

**The Bottom Line:** > The public should stop supporting a military system that is seen as corrupt and violent, as the cost of this system is paid by the taxpayers through ongoing injustice and war.
