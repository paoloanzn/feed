### Main Topic: Air Purification

**Main Thesis:** While far-UVC is effective, air filtration is currently the more practical and cost-effective solution for cleaning air in homes and large rooms.

**Simple Summary:** The article compares two ways to clean the air: using far-UVC light and using air filters. The author found that air filters are cheaper and quieter than far-UVC options, especially when considering the size of the room. In large spaces, filters become much more cost-effective, and noise levels are a major factor, making filters a better choice for most situations today.

**The Bottom Line:** > For most people, air filters offer a better balance of cost, noise, and performance than far-UVC technology right now.
