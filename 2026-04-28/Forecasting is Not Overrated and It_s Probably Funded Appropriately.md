### Main Topic: Forecasting Value

**Main Thesis:** Even if new forecasting funding doesn't offer massive financial returns, the infrastructure built by these efforts provides significant, often unquantifiable, benefits to society and knowledge.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that while spending large amounts of money on forecasting platforms might not yield huge financial profits, the work has created valuable knowledge infrastructure. These platforms allow many people to learn how to think critically and make better decisions, and they provide a way to check out false information. The author suggests that funding should focus on new, cutting-edge research rather than old forecasting methods, and that the social value of these communities is also important.

**The Bottom Line:** > The value of forecasting lies less in the money it generates and more in the knowledge and critical thinking it enables, making the infrastructure itself highly valuable.
