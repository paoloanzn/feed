### Main Topic: AI Advocacy

**Main Thesis:** To stop dangerous AI development, people must use courage and organize effectively to push for international agreements and policy changes.

**Simple Summary:** The author attended a conference focused on AI and learned how to effectively advocate for change. This involved organizing public protests, making signs, and lobbying politicians to support an international treaty to halt the development of powerful AI. The experience showed that success requires speaking truthfully, working with allies, and following up persistently with lawmakers.

**The Bottom Line:** > Concerned citizens must find the courage to speak up and join a growing movement to demand action on AI safety before it is too late.
