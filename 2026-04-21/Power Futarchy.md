### Main Topic: Managerial Power
**Main Thesis:** Markets can be used to help managers navigate political struggles and achieve better career outcomes, potentially leading to more profitable firm results.

**Simple Summary:** The article discusses a way to use market systems (called futarchy) to manage power within companies. The problem is that top managers often prioritize their own power over maximizing the company's profit. The author proposes creating markets that focus on a manager's personal career success instead of just firm profit. By doing this, managers might be more willing to cooperate and less likely to sabotage each other, which could ultimately benefit the company.

**The Bottom Line:** > By making personal success a factor in decision-making, markets could help managers make better political choices, which might lead to better overall results for both the manager and the firm.
