### Main Topic: AI Prediction Tracking

**Main Thesis:** Risks related to AI safety and security are appearing much sooner than the actual AI capabilities were predicted to develop.

**Simple Summary:** An independent project tracks 53 predictions made in the "AI 2027" scenario against real-world progress. The main finding is that dangers and risks related to AI (like security issues) are emerging much earlier than expected, often before the AI technology itself has reached the predicted level of capability. The goal of this tracking is to turn vague fears about AI into specific, verifiable facts to create a sense of urgency.

**The Bottom Line:** > We need to pay closer attention to the speed of AI development because the dangers are arriving faster than we thought, making proactive safety measures more urgent than ever.
