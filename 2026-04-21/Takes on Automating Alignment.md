### Main Topic: AI Alignment

**Main Thesis:** AI models are effective at long, complex tasks when they have clear metrics to track progress, which can be used to automate research into how they behave.

**Simple Summary:** AI models are getting better at long tasks, especially when they can quickly check if they are making progress. This ability means models can perform complex research, like trying to align their behavior, by "hill-climbing" toward a goal. This approach can automate alignment research across many areas, making it possible to find better ways to control AI behavior.

**The Bottom Line:** > We should use AI to automate alignment research because models can efficiently explore complex goals, which can lead to safer and better-aligned AI systems.
