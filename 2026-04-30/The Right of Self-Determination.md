### Main Topic: Self-Determination

**Main Thesis:** People living in a territory have the right to choose which state they want to belong to.

**Simple Summary:** Self-determination means that the people living in a specific area have the power to decide what kind of government or state they want to live under.

**The Bottom Line:** > This principle recognizes that the people themselves should have the final say over their own political future and governance.
