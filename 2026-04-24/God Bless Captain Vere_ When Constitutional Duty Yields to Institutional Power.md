### Main Topic: Law vs. Power

**Main Thesis:** People should remember that following the law is more important than obeying demands from government institutions.

**Simple Summary:** The article argues that when government institutions demand that people follow orders, they should not ignore the fundamental rules of the law. It uses the example of Captain Vere to show that individual duty and constitutional law must always come before the power of the government.

**The Bottom Line:** > You should always prioritize following the law over obeying demands from powerful institutions.
