### Main Topic: AI Policy

**Main Thesis:** People without technical skills can effectively reduce AI risk by engaging policymakers with clear, concrete, and credible arguments.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that instead of worrying about AI risks alone, people should take action by talking to politicians. To make an impact, you must act quickly, be credible (dress professionally and use simple facts), and present specific, actionable policy ideas rather than just general fears. The key is to use simple logic to convince lawmakers that AI poses immediate dangers that need regulation now.

**The Bottom Line:** > To make a real difference in managing AI risk, you must stop worrying and start taking concrete steps to influence the laws that govern the technology.
