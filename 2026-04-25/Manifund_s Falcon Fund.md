### Main Topic: AI Animal Welfare

**Main Thesis:** A new fund is being launched to find ways that transformative AI can ensure good outcomes for animals, bridging the gap between AI safety and animal welfare.

**Simple Summary:** This fund focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence and animal welfare. It aims to create tools and projects that address how AI development affects animals. This includes creating better ways to measure animal harm, developing rules (like constitutions) for AI models regarding animal treatment, and watching how industries use AI to protect animals.

**The Bottom Line:** > To ensure that powerful new AI technologies lead to positive results, we must actively focus on the welfare of animals as they develop.
